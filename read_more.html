What is RecoMed?

RecoMed is an AI-driven personal health companion that securely stores, organizes, and analyzes your medical history—turning scattered records into smart, actionable recommendations.

With RecoMed, users can effortlessly upload prescriptions, lab reports, and medical documents. Using advanced OCR and NLP, the system intelligently extracts key medical data—diagnoses, medications, allergies, and more—and builds a structured, searchable health timeline.

RecoMed doesn’t just hold your history; it understands it. Through machine learning, it detects trends in your health, predicts potential risks, and delivers personalized insights and reminders—so you can take control of your well-being with confidence and clarity.

Key Features

  • 📄 Intelligent parsing of medical documents (PDFs, images)
  • 📊 Dynamic health timeline with condition tracking
  • 🔍 Predictive insights for early risk detection
  • 💊 Smart medication and appointment reminders
  • 🔐 Encrypted, privacy-first health record storage
